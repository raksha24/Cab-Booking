Before the Trip

Getting a safe ride


Safe pickups
The Luxury Ride automatically finds your location to provide door-to-door service. That means you stay safe and comfortable wherever you are until your driver arrives.
Open to everyone, everywhere
All ride requests are blindly matched with the closest available driver. So there is no discrimination based on race, gender, or destination.
LuxuryRide profiles
When you are matched with a cab, you will see its name, license plate number, photo. And even after the trip, you are able to contact our support no. if you left something behind.

After the Trip

Always here for you


Feedback
After every trip, you can rate the driver and provide feedback about your ride. We review all feedback because our goal is to make every ride a great experience.
24/7 support
Our support team is always ready to respond to any questions you may have about your trip and help you retrieve lost items.
Rapid response
Our specially-trained incident response teams are available around the clock to handle any urgent concerns that arise.
